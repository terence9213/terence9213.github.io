HOW TO PLAY

Eternity Quest is a straight forward top-down shooter.

Controls can be found in the settings menu, where you can toggle between mouse and keyboard control. You may also toggle sound effects and music in the settings menu.

In the inventory menu, you may purchase and upgrade your primary and secondary weapons. The first weapon, the "Blaster" can be purchased for free. You may equip 2 primary weapons and 1 secondary weapon to your loadout.

When you start the game, you will encounter waves of enemies and a boss enemy at every 3rd level. Enemy health, speed and spawning interval will increase gradually as you progress. A collision with an enemy, a boss, or the projectiles fired by the boss will result in instant death.

Most weapons have an overheat and cooldown timer. When a weapon overheats, it will become disabled until it has fully cooled down.

You can move the ship with either the mouse of the directional keys. (Mouse control is highly recommended)

All settings and game data are saved in your browser cookies, and clearing your cookies will result in losing saved data.

IMPORTANT:

Please note that this game does not work on mobile browsers. Safari users may also face some issues. The current version is most compatible with Chrome.
